About Our Church

A Community of Believers on a Mission

Christ Winners Assembly is more than a church — it is a family. Founded under the visionary leadership of Reverend Samuel Ojediran, we have grown into a thriving community dedicated to spreading the Gospel and transforming lives.

Our mission is to raise a generation of winners who are empowered by the Word of God, equipped for service, and inspired to impact their communities with love and excellence.

Ministries

There's a Place for Everyone

From children to seniors, discover a ministry that fits your season of life and calling.

Children's Ministry

Fun, safe, and spiritually enriching programs for children ages 3–12. We believe in raising the next generation of godly leaders.

Youth & Young Adults

Dynamic programs for teens and young adults focusing on discipleship, career, relationships, and purpose-driven living.

Marriage & Family

Building strong, Christ-centered marriages and families through counseling, retreats, and support groups.

Missions & Outreach

Local and global outreach initiatives to spread the Gospel and provide relief to communities in need.
