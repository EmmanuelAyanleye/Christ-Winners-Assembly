Service Times

We gather throughout the week to worship, learn, and pray together. There's always a reason to join us.

Sunday Worship Service

Our flagship weekly gathering featuring praise & worship, prayer, and anointed teaching. Holy Communion is served on the first Sunday of each month.

Our Ministries

Discover where you can use your gifts to serve God and bless others.

Children's Church

Ages 3–12. Fun Bible lessons, games, and activities in a safe, supervised environment.

Youth Ministry (CWA Youth)

Ages 13–25. Weekly meetings, retreats, and mentorship for the next generation.

Marriage & Family

Counseling, enrichment programs, and family retreats to build Christ-centered homes.

Men of Valor

A brotherhood of men committed to spiritual growth, accountability, and leadership in their homes.

Daughters of Zion

A dynamic women's ministry empowering ladies through prayer, fellowship, and skill development.

Missions & Outreach

Local and global missions, food drives, medical outreaches, and evangelism programs.

Praise & Worship

Our music ministry leads the congregation in Spirit-filled worship during services and special events.

Media & Tech

Sound, video, livestream, and social media teams ensuring the message reaches far and wide.
