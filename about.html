Our Story

From a Small Gathering to a Mighty Movement

Christ Winners Assembly was founded in 2005 by Reverend Samuel Ojediran with a handful of faithful believers meeting in a living room. What began as a small prayer gathering has blossomed into a global ministry touching thousands of lives across nations.

Through unwavering faith, consistent prayer, and the teaching of God's Word, the church has grown into a vibrant community of over 3,500 members, with branches in multiple cities and countries.

Our journey has been marked by miracles, transformation, and the relentless pursuit of God's purpose for every individual who walks through our doors.

Our Mission

To raise a generation of winners empowered by the Word of God, equipped for ministry, and committed to transforming their communities through the love and power of Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

To become a global lighthouse of faith, raising leaders, planting churches, and impacting nations until every person has experienced the saving grace of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Leadership

Meet Our Pastor

Guided by faith, driven by purpose, and committed to serving God's people.

Reverend Samuel Ojediran

Founder & Senior Pastor

Reverend Samuel Ojediran is a visionary leader, teacher, and spiritual father with over 25 years of ministry experience. He holds a Master's degree in Theology and has ministered across Africa, Europe, and North America.

Known for his practical teaching style and prophetic insight, Reverend Ojediran has helped thousands discover their purpose in Christ. He is passionate about leadership development, community transformation, and raising the next generation of ministers.

He is happily married to Pastor Mrs. Grace Ojediran, and together they are blessed with three children. They reside in Lagos, Nigeria, where they continue to serve the body of Christ with devotion and joy.

Our Journey

Milestones Through the Years

A timeline of God's faithfulness in the life of our church.

2005 — The Beginning

Christ Winners Assembly was founded by Reverend Samuel Ojediran with 12 members meeting in a living room.

2008 — First Building

The church acquired its first permanent worship center, accommodating 300 people.

2012 — Branch Expansion

Launched our first branch church in Abuja, Nigeria, marking the beginning of our multi-site vision.

2016 — Global Missions

Established missionary partnerships in Ghana, Kenya, and the United Kingdom.

2020 — Digital Transformation

Expanded our reach through online streaming, touching thousands of viewers weekly across the globe.

2026 — Today

A thriving community of over 3,500 members with 15 ministries, 5 branches, and a global audience.
